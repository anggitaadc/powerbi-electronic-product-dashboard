# 📊 Electronic Product Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive sales dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI to transform raw transactional data of electronic products into actionable business insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor performance, identify trends, and support data-driven decision making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
- Analyze sales performance of electronic products across multiple dimensions (time, product, geography, and customer segments)
- Identify top-performing electronic product categories
- Understand customer purchasing behavior and payment preferences
- Support strategic decisions to optimize revenue growth

---

## 📊 Key Features
- **Time Series Analysis**: Monthly sales trends highlighting seasonality and peak periods  
- **Customer Segmentation**: Insights based on age group and gender distribution  
- **Product Performance**: Contribution analysis across electronic product categories  
- **Geographical Insights**: Sales comparison across countries  
- **Payment Behavior Analysis**: Distribution of transaction methods  
- **Seller Performance**: Evaluation of individual seller contributions  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Sales show a strong upward trend toward **Q4**, with peak performance in **December**, indicating seasonal demand  
- Electronic products such as *Air Conditioners* and *Televisions* are major revenue contributors  
- Countries like *Belgium* and *Philippines* demonstrate strong market performance  
- Cash and digital payments are the most preferred transaction methods
